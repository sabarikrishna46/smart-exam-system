👨‍🏫 Invigilator Assignment

Efficient exam monitoring begins with the right invigilator allocation. Our Invigilator Assignment feature empowers administrators to seamlessly assign faculty members to examination rooms based on their availability, department, and exam schedules.

  • 📅 View all upcoming exams with date, time, and room information
  • 🧑‍🏫 Assign invigilators to specific exam sessions
  • 🔁 Modify or reassign duties quickly when needed
  • 📄 Export assignments to PDF or print for coordination
  • 🚫 Prevent double-bookings with automatic conflict detection

This ensures every exam is properly supervised, reducing manual errors and improving administrative efficiency.
